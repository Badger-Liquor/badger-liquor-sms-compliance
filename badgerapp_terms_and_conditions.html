Eligibility

The SMS notification service is available exclusively to:

  • Active Badger Liquor Company employees with a valid BadgerApp account.
  • Authorized field sales representatives assigned within BadgerApp.
  • Individuals with a U.S. mobile phone number capable of receiving SMS messages.

This service is not available to the general public and is not used for consumer marketing or promotional purposes.

4

Opt-In & Opt-Out

To Opt In: Navigate to Notification Preferences in your BadgerApp profile settings. Enter your mobile number, review the consent disclosure, and confirm your opt-in. Alternatively, text START, JOIN, or BADGER to the program toll-free number.

To Opt Out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re-enroll. You may also disable SMS in your BadgerApp Notification Settings.

Help: Reply HELP to any message to receive assistance information, or contact IT support directly.

Opting out of SMS notifications does not affect your BadgerApp account or employment status. All operational information remains accessible within the application.
