Overview

Badger Liquor Company ("Badger Liquor," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our employees, authorized sales representatives, and any individuals whose information we process in connection with our internal business applications, including BadgerApp.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information, including mobile phone numbers used for our SMS notification service.

2

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, business phone number, and mobile phone number.
  • Employment Information: Employee ID, job title, sales territory, and assigned customer accounts.
  • Application Usage Data: Login timestamps, feature usage, order activity, and device information associated with BadgerApp sessions.
  • SMS Consent Records: Date, time, and method by which you consented to receive SMS notifications, including your opt-in keyword or in-app consent action.
3

SMS Messaging Program

Badger Liquor Company operates an SMS notification service through BadgerApp, delivered via Microsoft Azure Communication Services. Participation is voluntary and available to authorized employees and field sales representatives only.

By opting in to SMS notifications, you consent to receive recurring text messages regarding order status, delivery updates, customer credit alerts, and other operational notifications related to your role at Badger Liquor Company.
  • How to Opt In: Enable SMS notifications within your BadgerApp profile settings and confirm your mobile number and consent.
  • How to Opt Out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time, or disable SMS in your BadgerApp notification settings. You will receive a final confirmation message.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message or contact IT support at it@badgerliquor.com.
  • Message Frequency: Varies based on operational activity. Typically 1–10 messages per business day per user.
  • Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier plan. Badger Liquor Company does not charge for SMS messages.
  • Carrier Disclaimer: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • No Data Sold: We do not sell, share, or transfer your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data to third parties for marketing purposes.
4

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering SMS notifications you have opted in to receive.
  • Authenticating users and maintaining the security of BadgerApp.
  • Supporting order management, route planning, and sales operations.
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations.
  • Improving internal application functionality and user experience.
5

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors (such as Microsoft Azure) who process data on our behalf under contractual data protection obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Operations: Within Badger Liquor Company as necessary for operational purposes.

Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in data are never shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

6

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information, including encrypted data transmission, access controls, and regular security reviews of our Azure-hosted infrastructure.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information using reasonable safeguards.

Your Rights

Employees and authorized users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting our IT department. SMS opt-out requests are honored immediately upon receipt of a STOP reply or in-app setting change.
